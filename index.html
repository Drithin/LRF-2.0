About

Mr K. K. Raja, the founder trustee of Latha Raja Foundation (LRF) along with his late Mrs K. S. S. Latha used to donate up to 80% of their earnings towards the welfare of the society through many events and ventures that support numerous social causes in Telugu states of Telangana and Andhra Pradesh. After the demise of his beloved wife, Mr K. K. Raja, with the noble intention to streamline and extend the reach of these charity activities has laid the foundation for LRF, assembling a group of six like-minded, spirited, and socially responsible humanitarians as trustees.
Through LRF, Mr. K. K. Raja and six trustees intend to contribute towards betterment of society by various activities such as providing financial assistance for education of underprivileged and talented students, engage in activities to improve the health of poor, support organizations and individuals who relentlessly work on social causes, provide relief assistance to troubled parties of any natural disaster or other calamities, provide financial support to enrich Indian literature, safeguard and promote Indian culture and heritage, provide financial support to the downtrodden and underdeveloped citizens etc.
